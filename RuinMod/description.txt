                    Ruin is a content mod for Terraria. It has a total of 
                    91 new modded items and 64 new crafting recipes! 

                    A more specific and detailed list of what Ruin Mod adds:
                    - +91 modded items!
                    - +1 new modded class!
                    - +9 armor sets!
                    - +59 weapons
                    - +3 enemy NPCS!
